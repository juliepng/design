Website—UnionPay Laboratory

Overview

This website belong to China UnionPay. Its aim is to build a first-class and internationally renowned research base for electronic commerce and electronic payment technology in China, become an important source of independent innovation in electronic commerce and electronic payment in China, further improve China's strategic position in the field of electronic payment, and enhance the independent innovation capability and core competitiveness of the industry.

Skills

Role

Responsible for web design and experience. In the early stage, Responsible for the interaction of the entire website and reports to the project manager . In the later stage, Design the entire interface submit to the developers.



Challenges

Before starting the project, our team went to UnionPay Studio to communicate with relevant personnel in charge of the project, with a general direction and framework logic.
  • 1. Remote operation, we work separately from the customers. In order to keep the schedule after the plan is determined, many sets of design plans will be brought to communicate with the customers to exchange our ideas and problems.
  • 2.Internal scheduling and communication. At the beginning of the project, our project manager made a timetable to ensure the work content of subsequent development and the whole project cycle. At the same time, customers can also intuitively feel the progress of the project.
  • 3.Considering website scalability.
